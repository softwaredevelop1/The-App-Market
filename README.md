# The App Market

Welcome to the app market, a place where you can get apps for your iPhone!

Be careful! While apps are generally safe, its important to remember that these apps may break on your device, so install at your own risk!

# How to set it up:
1. Install either Sidestore or Altstore on your device. I recommend Altstore because altstore has auto refresh, meaning your apps don't suddenly stop working. Apple has a 7 day app limit on the free plan.
2. Launch Sidestore/Altstore and go to Sources
3. In Sources, type in this url: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/softwaredevelop1/The-App-Market/refs/heads/main/apps.json
4. You should see my source appear, feel free to look around and install apps as you wish!

# Things to remember
 - Make sure that you refresh the app constantly to prevent it from expiring!
 - You can only have 3 apps at a time with the free plan. Sorry!

Thanks for using The App Market!
